Democracy works best when everyone participates. There are many ways you can get involved with the Mendocino County Democratic Party and make a difference in your community.

## Volunteer

We always need enthusiastic volunteers to help with:
- **Voter Registration Drives** — Help fellow citizens register to vote
- **Phone Banking & Canvassing** — Reach out to voters during election season
- **Event Planning** — Help organize our meetings, fundraisers, and community events
- **Social Media** — Help spread the word about our candidates and issues online
- **Office Support** — Administrative tasks that keep our operation running smoothly

## Attend Our Meetings

Our Central Committee holds monthly meetings that are open to all registered Democrats. It's a great way to meet like-minded people, hear from candidates, and stay informed about local political issues.

## Run for Office

Interested in running for local office? We support and mentor Democratic candidates at all levels. Whether you're considering a run for school board, city council, county supervisor, or another position, we're here to help.

## Join a Democratic Club

Mendocino County has several chartered Democratic clubs that meet regularly. Find one near you on our Chartered Clubs page and get connected with Democrats in your area.

## Register to Vote

If you're not already registered, or if you've moved recently, make sure your voter registration is up to date. Visit [RegisterToVote.ca.gov](https://registertovote.ca.gov/) to register or update your information.

## Stay Informed

- Follow us on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/Mendodems/)
- Attend our events and meetings
- Check our website regularly for news and updates

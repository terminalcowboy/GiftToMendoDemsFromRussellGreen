We are a group of people who share progressive democratic values. As a political party, we work to promote these ideals in Mendocino County through our elected officials and by encouraging political, social, and environmental issues that reflect our vision.

## The Central Committee

The Mendocino County Democratic Central Committee (MCDP) is the official local arm of the California Democratic Party. Our committee is made up of elected and appointed members who are passionate about making a difference in our community.

## What We Do

- **Support Democratic Candidates:** We endorse and support Democratic candidates running for local, state, and federal office in Mendocino County.
- **Voter Outreach:** We register voters, provide information about candidates and ballot measures, and work to increase voter turnout.
- **Community Engagement:** We host events, forums, and meetings to keep our community informed and engaged in the democratic process.
- **Advocacy:** We advocate for policies that reflect our progressive values, from environmental protection to social justice.

## Our History

The Mendocino County Democratic Central Committee has been serving our community for decades, working to ensure that the voices of Mendocino County residents are heard at every level of government. From the redwood forests to the rugged coastline, we represent the diverse communities that make up our beautiful county.

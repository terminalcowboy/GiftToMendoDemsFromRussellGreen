We're thrilled to unveil the new website for the Mendocino County Democratic Central Committee! Our redesigned site makes it easier than ever to stay informed about local Democratic activities, find your elected officials, and get involved in your community.

## What's New

- **Modern Design:** A clean, responsive design that works great on phones, tablets, and computers
- **Events Calendar:** Stay up-to-date with meetings, volunteer opportunities, and community events
- **Blog & News:** Regular updates on local Democratic Party activities and political news
- **Easy Donations:** Multiple ways to support our work, including PayPal and ActBlue
- **Get Involved:** Find volunteer opportunities and learn how to make a difference

## Stay Connected

Follow us on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/Mendodems/) and check back regularly for the latest news and events. Together, we're building a stronger, more progressive Mendocino County.

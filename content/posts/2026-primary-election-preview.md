The June 2026 primary election is approaching, and there are several important races that will impact Mendocino County. Here's what you need to know.

## Key Dates

- **Voter Registration Deadline:** May 18, 2026
- **Vote-by-Mail Ballots Sent:** Starting May 4, 2026
- **Primary Election Day:** June 2, 2026

## How to Participate

- Make sure your voter registration is current at [RegisterToVote.ca.gov](https://registertovote.ca.gov/)
- Volunteer with the MCDP to help get out the vote
- Attend candidate forums hosted by local Democratic clubs

Stay tuned for endorsement announcements from the Mendocino County Democratic Central Committee.

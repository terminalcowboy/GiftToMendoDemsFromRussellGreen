## Why Donate?

The Democratic Party of Mendocino County relies on the generosity of supporters like you to fund our work. Your donations help us:

- **Register Voters** and increase civic participation
- **Support Democratic Candidates** running for local office
- **Organize Events** that bring our community together
- **Communicate Our Message** to voters across the county
- **Fight for Progressive Values** at every level of government

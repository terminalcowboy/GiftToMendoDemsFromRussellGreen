---
title: "What We Stand For"
subtitle: "Our Values and Platform"
---

The Mendocino County Democratic Party stands for the values that make our communities strong, our environment healthy, and our democracy vibrant. We believe in an America where everyone has the opportunity to succeed.

## Environment & Climate Action

Mendocino County's stunning natural beauty — from ancient redwood forests to our pristine coastline — is at the heart of who we are. We support aggressive action on climate change, protection of our natural resources, transitioning to clean energy, and sustainable land use practices that preserve our environment for future generations.

## Healthcare for All

Every person in Mendocino County deserves access to quality, affordable healthcare. We support expanding healthcare coverage, lowering prescription drug costs, protecting reproductive rights, and strengthening mental health and substance abuse treatment services in our rural communities.

## Economic Justice

We believe in an economy that works for working families, not just the wealthy. We support living wages, workers' rights to organize, small business development, affordable housing, and investments in infrastructure that create good-paying local jobs.

## Public Education

Strong public schools are the foundation of our democracy. We support fully funded public education from pre-K through college, fair pay for teachers, and access to vocational training and higher education without crushing student debt.

## Social Justice & Civil Rights

We stand for equality and justice for all people regardless of race, gender, sexual orientation, gender identity, religion, or national origin. We oppose discrimination in all its forms and support criminal justice reform, voting rights, and immigration reform that treats all people with dignity.

## Democracy & Good Government

We believe in transparent, accountable government that serves the people. We support campaign finance reform, voting rights protections, and ending gerrymandering. We oppose voter suppression and fight to ensure every eligible citizen can exercise their right to vote.

## Rural Communities

As a rural county, we understand the unique challenges facing our communities. We support expanding broadband access, investing in rural healthcare, protecting family farms and ranches, and ensuring that rural voices are heard in Sacramento and Washington.

## Gun Safety

We support common-sense gun safety measures that protect our communities while respecting responsible gun ownership. We believe we can reduce gun violence through universal background checks, red flag laws, and research into gun violence prevention.
